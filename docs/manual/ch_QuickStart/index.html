Quick Start

Welcome to the site!

You may have arrived with different backgrounds and interest. The site is about neuron-based computing, which, for today may mean very different topics. The primary goal is to deal with biologically implemented neurons (they are created ’as is’; maybe not entirely understood, but attempting to discover it) and also artificially manufactured neurons which attempt to recreate the biological ones, grasping one or more of their true features; furthermore, their networks, operations, features and fallacies.

Anyhow, you will need to know that the college level of physics is usually not sufficient to understand the deepness of the material and you will need to re-read the notions; the more you know it (and especially the more biophysics you learned) the more carefully. A half-understanding of the physical base hinders your learning.
