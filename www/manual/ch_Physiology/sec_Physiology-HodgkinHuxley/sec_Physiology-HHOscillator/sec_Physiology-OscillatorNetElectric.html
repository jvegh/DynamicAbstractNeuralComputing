2.1.1 Oscillator type2.1.1 Oscillator type2.2 The physical model

HH measured [5] the time course of the neuronal membrane when clamping axonal vvolgae on and off. The meticulously observed and measured that the time contants τ of the exponential charge-up and discharge processes are different. On the one side, they underpinned that our equations 3.27 and 3.26 describe correctly the axonal chargeup current and discharge currents. On the other, as they noticed that the time constants of the two processes are significantly different (see the green and the dashed blue diagram lines), underpinning that the axonal charge production mechanism significantly changes the axonal source current. The green line has significantly slower rise since the axonal current only gradually increases after switching the clamping on. Without that current change, the charge-up current would follow the blue line.
