§ 3.5.3 Two segments

We can separate the volume into two segments by a thin isolating membrane. The membrane is thin; we assume the separating membrane is transparent for the electric interaction (the electric field affects the ions in the other segment on the other side of the membrane) but not for their masses (mechanically separates the segments).
